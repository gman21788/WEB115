Guinness Beef Stew

Ingredients
6 slices of bacon (thick cut) 2.5 pounds stew beef or beef chuck
2 yellow onions 4 cloves garlic
1 14.9oz Guinness (or Murphy's) .25 cup tomato paste
tsp dried thyme 6 carrots
salt and pepper to taste and season 2-3 cups chicken stock
Directions
  • Prepare ingredients by slicing bacon, beef (if required), onions, and carrots.
  • Saute bacon in heavy bottomed skillet or dutch oven over med-high heat until crispy.
  • Transfer bacon to a separate container, but reserve fat.
  • Season beef generously with salt and pepper. Sear each piece on both sides until browned.
    Transfer to container with bacon.
  • Saute onion in remaining bacon fat and beef drippings until tender, season with salt to taste.
  • Add garlic and cook with onions until aromatic. Pour in beer and use a wooden spoon to scrape
    up drippings.
  • Add remaining ingredients, including beef and bacon, and just barely cover with chicken stock.
  • Bring stew to simmer, stir to combine. Cook covered until beef is fork tender, about 2-3 hours.
  • Remove cover and cook on med-high for 15-20 minutes to thicken stew. Season to taste.
  • Serve hot with mashed potatoes (optional)
Original recipe by Chef John.
