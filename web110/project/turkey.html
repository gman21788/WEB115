Thanksgiving Turkey

Ingredients
1 turkey of any size 1 large yellow onion, halved
1 lemon, halved; 2 lemons for juicing or lemon juice 10 cloves of garlic, peeled
1 pound bacon 1 stick butter
3 tbsp olive oil 1 bunch flat leaf parsely, chopped
salt and pepper to taste and season
Directions
  • Prepare a compound butter by combining butter, 1 tbsp olive oil, parsley, the juice of 2 lemons, 2 cloves of garlic,
    and salt and pepper to season.
  • Remove giblets from turkey, and stuff turkey with halved onion and lemon as well as 8 cloves of garlic.
  • Use your fingers to loosen skin from turkey and pack butter underneath skin. Put extra butter on top of turkey.
  • Use remaining 2 tbsp olive oil to spread over top of the skin, which will help prevent the butter from burning.
  • Sprinkle salt and pepper over top of the turkey.
  • Lay bacon over top of the turkey to act as a "shield," which will help prevent turkey from drying out.
  • Roast turkey at 350°F until the thickest part of the leg is a temperature of 165°F.
  • Remove from oven and allow turkey to rest for at least 45 minutes.
  • Reserve drippings, bacon, halved onion, halved lemons, and halved garlic. These will go great in your gravy.
Original recipe by Chef Gordon Ramsey.
