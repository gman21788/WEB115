Hot Maryland Crab Dip

Ingredients
1 pound Maryland crab meat (don't use claws) 2 8oz packages cream cheese
1 8oz container sour cream 4 heaping tbsp mayonnaise
1/2 - 2/3 cup lemon juice (but start with 1/2 cup) 3-4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
3 tsp dry mustard 3 shakes garlic salt
1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese (half in, half on top) Old Bay to taste
Directions
  • Preheat oven to 325Â°F
  • Combine ingredients together, reserving half of the cheddar cheese.
  • Mix with stand mixer if possible, otherwise stir as needed.
  • Taste & adjust as needed.
  • Sprinkle top of dip with half of the cheddar cheese.
  • Bake in preheated oven in dutch oven for 30min or until golden brown on top and hot.
