Site Planning Analysis Page


Website Goal

  • The goal of my website is to show off what I love to do most... cook!
  • Readers who visit my website should feel empowered to get into their kitchen and get their hands dirty (after they wash them of course!).
  • I think that too often, home cooks can be scared to try something new, but learning to cook things that you aren't comfortable with is what makes you better.

Working Titles of Pages

  • Home
  • Blog
  • Recipe Page(s) - 2 or 3 recipes, one per page
  • Challenge Recipes - This page will have a form that viewers can submit their own recipes to for me to attempt to recreate, because learning new things makes you better in the kitchen!
  • About

Information Requirements

Most of the text information, I already have. They're my recipes after all. My pictures will likely be in-process and finished pictures of each recipe, which will all be original.

I don't expect to include many sounds or videos to start out with. I don't think those two types of content will add anything meaningful to the site when it's first starting out.

Challenge Recipes

I think allowing visitors to submit their own recipes for me to try will add a sense of engagement to the website. It'll also show that you can cook anything you put your mind to, you just have to try!

The form would need to include:

  • A space for an ingredient list (with measurements).
  • A place to put recipe instructions, which should include timing.
  • User's contact information.
  • Any additional recipe notes or hints.
